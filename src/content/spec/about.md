Hey, I'm **Damir** — a Systems engineer & team lead based in the UK, and this is my corner of the internet.

> Named after the Linux pseudo-random number generator, because my interests work in roughly the same way.

## About

I've been running this blog under various forms since the mid-2010s. It started as a place to dump notes, and slowly evolved into something I'm actually proud of.

I write here because I spend a lot of time figuring things out the hard way, and turning that into a post is the cheapest form of documentation I know. If something took me three hours to debug, it should take you three minutes to fix.

## What I Write About

Mostly infrastructure, reliability, and the kind of homelab rabbit holes that start with *"this will take an hour"* and end three weekends later. Expect a mix of deep technical write-ups and honest opinions on tooling.

`Kubernetes` · `Terraform` · `CI/CD` · `Cloudflare` · `Tailscale` · `Self-hosted` · `Docker` · `Linux` · `SRE` · `Productivity`
